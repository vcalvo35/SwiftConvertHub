About SwiftConvertHub

SwiftConvertHub is a free, fast, and accurate unit converter built for everyone — students, engineers, developers, travelers, and curious minds.

We built SwiftConvertHub because unit conversion should be simple and instant. No sign-up forms, no cluttered interfaces, no paywalls. Just open the tool and convert.

What We Offer

  • Length & Distance — meters, kilometers, miles, feet, inches and more
  • Weight & Mass — kilograms, pounds, ounces, stones, tonnes
  • Temperature — Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin, Rankine
  • Digital Storage — bytes, KB, MB, GB, TB, PB and binary prefixes
  • Binary & Number Systems — decimal, binary, hexadecimal, octal

Our Promise

SwiftConvertHub will always be free. We may display non-intrusive ads to keep the lights on, but we will never require an account, never sell your data, and never put converters behind a paywall.
