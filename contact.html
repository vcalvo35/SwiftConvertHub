Contact SwiftConvertHub

SwiftConvertHub is built and maintained by one person with the goal of making unit conversion fast, accurate, and free for everyone. Whether you spotted a bug, have an idea for a new converter, or just want to share feedback — your message goes directly to the person who built this site.

What Can We Help With?

  • Bug Report — a converter is giving wrong results
  • New Converter — a unit or category you'd like to see
  • Content Feedback — an article or explanation to improve
  • General Question — anything about the site or conversions
  • Other — anything else

We typically respond within 2–3 business days.
